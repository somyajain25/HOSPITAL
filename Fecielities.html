:::: Facilities ::::

  • Vidhaya Sagar Hospital is running under the North Delhi Municipal Corporation under the supervision of Director Hospital Administration & Guidance of Addl. Commissioner (Health).

  • It has indoor and outdoor facilities in Gynae,Medicine and Pediatrics Dept.,PPTCT,VTCT,SENTINAL SURVEILLANCES(for diagnosis and management of ADIS and DOT’s centre(for treatment of tuberculosis)) are fully operational. A part from Allopathy Ayurvedic dispensary is conducted daily.

  • Gynae Dept has modern keyhole surgery equipment’s and Diagnostic facilities by Hysteroscopic Colposcopic techniques. It is the only center in MCD performing recanalization Operation for infertility.

  • Pediatric Dept. has the best Nursery in MCD with thirty bedded modern intensive care facilities for the care of new born and is equipped with all life support systems and monitoring devices along with central suction and oxygen facilities.

  • Thalassemia Day care center is running by collaborative of blood bank and pediatrics and Pathology. Physiotherapy and Dental Dept. have recently been upgraded and take care of OPD and indoor patients.
