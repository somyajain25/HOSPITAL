Fellowship in National Boards (FNB) Course

DNB/FNB is recognized qualifications awarded by National Board of Examinations(NBE).Our institute is accredited by National Board of Examinations(NBE)since january 2018 for DNB in various Broad and Supar specialties & FNB course. The nomenclature of the qualification awarded by the National Board of Examinations is 'Diplomate of National Board'.The list of recognised qulifications awarded by the Board in various Broad and Supar specialities as approved by the Goverment of india and included in the First schedule of IMC Act 1956 is as at annexure. The diplomate/fellowship qulification awarded by the National Board of Examinations (NBE) are equated with the postgraduate and post doctorate degrees awarded by other indian Medical universities.
